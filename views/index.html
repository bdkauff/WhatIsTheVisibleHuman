What Is The Visible Human? is a project that explores the power of images—in this case, images of the human body. The sheer ubiquity of bodily imagery in the media, as well as our own perception of self, breeds a rich language of description and critique, but also simplification and categorization.

Inspired by MRI slices obtained from the Visible Human Project, I wanted to know: what happens when we see our body from a different angle? I submitted both the MRI images as well as “superficial” images of the human body to Amazon’s Mechanical Turk and asked people, “What is the Visible Human?”

Made by Ben Kauffman.
